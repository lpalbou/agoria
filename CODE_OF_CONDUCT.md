# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in Agoria to be a harassment-free, respectful experience
for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and considerate.
- Welcome newcomers and differing viewpoints; critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith and prefer clarity over point-scoring.
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests, and
any community channels — and to public spaces when representing the project.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the maintainer via the contact in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports will be reviewed and handled discreetly and
fairly. The maintainer may remove, edit, or reject contributions and
participation that violate this code.
